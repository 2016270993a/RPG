A Note on Skills

  • Dark Age specific skills are listed below the standard V5 skills. For our sessions, we wanted to prioritize V5 over Dark Age and reduce any duplication. This meant that some skills are not included due to them being renamed. Additionally, some skills could be adjusted to not be necessary, but were included to favor your own play-style.
  • Direct substitutions that were not included are as follows (DA to V5).
    • Alertness to Awareness: Renamed for V5.
    • Expression to Persusion: Renamed for V5.
    • Empathy to Insight: Renamed for V5.
    • Seneschal to Finance: Renamed for V5.
  • Skills contained only within Dark Age, possible substites and other notes.
    • Archery: V5 calls this out as used in Athletics. However, we decided to keep it separate, so as to not make Athletics overpowered for a potentially common skill. As our campaign will go from Dark Age to Modern, this skill will transition to Firearms which would also be a good substitute.
    • Commerce: Could be replaced by Finance, Persuasion, Streetwise, Subterfuge, etc. depending on how the character is trying to accomplish their action.
    • Enigmas: Could be replaced by Investigation, Occult, Hearth Wisdom, etc. depending on how the character is trying to accomplish their action. As this is often used in Werewolf games to commune with Fae, it otherwise merits its own entry
    • Hearth Wisdom: Kept separate as a baseline for peasant knowledge that falls short of Academics.
    • Law: Could be replaced by Academics, Finance, Leadership, Politics (the current ruler dictates the current laws), etc. depending on how the character is trying to accomplish their action. This is also prime to be taken as a specialty.
    • Legerdemain: Could be replaced by Larceny, Stealth, etc. depending on how the character is trying to accomplish their action.
    • Ride: Also potentially substituted by Athletics, we kept this separate due to common usage. Modern V5 would replaced this with Drive.
    • Theology: Potentially used as Academics with a Specialty. Heavy faith characters will appreciate the separate skill.
  • Skills only contained within V5.
    • Drive
    • Firearms
    • Science
    • Technology

Converting Attributes

  • In V5, Appearance and Perception no longer exist. Instead Composure and Resolve have taken their place. However, there is not an exact translation between them. We've found that you can perform some simple adjustments to an old character and translate them reasonably so.
  • Our approach substitues Courage one-to-one with Resolve. Then by taking the characters total Willpower score and subtracting Resolve/Courage, this results in the characters Composure. While there may be some tweaking for player characters, this has worked well for various NPCs listed in supplement books.
  • For example, Malachite from the Dark Ages has Courage 3 and Willpower 8. To convert him to V5 he is given a Resolve of 3 (direct substitute from Courage) and a Composure of 5 (Willpower 8 minus Courage 3).
  • While we have found the above approach useful when converting many NPCs from various supplemental books, your mileage may vary and are encourage to make any adjustments desired for your adventures.

Roads

  • As mentioned under 'Converting attributes' above, the Courage Virtue can be directly substituted for Resolve. Using that approach, only two Virtues are listed adjacent to Road as Courage is assumed to be Resolve.

Rolling

  • To find out exactly what dice have been rolled, hover the mouse over the number of successes. In rolls where the Hunger dice are used (Attribute, Ability, Discipline rolls) if you fail, check to see if you rolled a 1 on the Hunger dice. If this is the case then consult the rules to determine how to proceed (Page 207, 208).
  • On a roll, if the number of successes is green, you may have rolled a critical success. Hover the mouse over and count how many 10's a rolled to determine if you rolled a critical (Page 120) or a messy critical success (Page 206, 207). Please note, the extra 2 successes for a Crit are not automatically added.
  • Discipline rolls account for the Blood Potency bonus. (Page 216)

This sheet is designed to be compatible where appropriate. Entering values beyond the prior limitations (8 dots in a discipline) would require manual adjustment if going back. Otherwise the data values use the same names and should carry over.
