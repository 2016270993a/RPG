Basic and Special Moves
Scan the Environment
When you investigate the world around you, describe what sense, expertise, power, or skill you use to interact with your environment, and roll +Community. On a hit, your Guide will show you something about your world. On a 10+ you gain +1 Ongoing until the end of the scene when acting on the information you gain.
Redirect the Current
When you act to remove an obstacle in your path, roll +Calm. On a hit you succeed at your task and avoid trouble. On a 7-9 the Guide will give you a hard choice, introduce an instability, or enact a cost.
Reach Out
When you ask for help from nature or your community, roll +Hope. On a hit your pleas are heard. Describe how the natural world or members of your community come to your aid. On a 7-9 the Guide will describe how the help is temporary, unstable, or threatening
Defend Another
When you risk your own safety to protect another, roll +Altruism. On a hit you protect your ward. On a 7-9, choose two from the following list:
  • You take the hit
  • You end up in a bad spot
  • You lash out at a friend
On a miss, choose two from the above list, and your ward remains unprotected
Do Something Wondrous
When you use your gifts in a spectacular way, roll +Drive. On a hit, describe how you accomplished your goal. On a 7-9, choose one:
  • You suffer a backlash
  • You lose control at the worst time
  • You put others in danger
Salve Wounds
When you tend to another’s suffering, ask that player (or the Guide if it’s an NPC) if they accept your help. If the answer is yes, roll +Hope. On a hit, you successfully render aid.
If your target is a PC, they clear a Condition or hold 1 Maneuver.
If you are tending an NPC, you help them as indicated by the narrative and clear a Condition or a Corruption.
On a 10+, you also mark Harmony or hold 1 Maneuver.
